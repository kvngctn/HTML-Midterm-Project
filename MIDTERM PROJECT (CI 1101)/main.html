Welcome to LITS

The League of Information Technology Students (LITS) is the official student organization representing Information Technology students at the University of the East – Caloocan. Since its establishment in 2004, LITS has continuously provided opportunities for students to grow academically, technically, and professionally.

Through seminars, conventions, competitions, outreach programs, and collaborative activities, LITS creates a dynamic environment where students can develop leadership skills, expand their technical knowledge, and build meaningful connections within the IT community.

💡 What LITS Offers

  • ✓ Academic and technical seminars
  • ✓ Annual major events (IT Convention, DCSS Nights)
  • ✓ Competitive programming & gaming events
  • ✓ Leadership and officer opportunities
  • ✓ Campus-wide recognition and excellence
  • ✓ Strong student community

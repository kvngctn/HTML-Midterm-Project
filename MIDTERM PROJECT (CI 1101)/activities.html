🩵 Mr. & Ms. LITS 2025
Congratulations to our Mr. and Ms. LITS, Paulhan Raphael Cacnio and Bless Jhiniel Selda, who proudly represented the League of Information Technology Students at Mr. and Ms. Engineering 2025 on October 21, 2025.

- Best in Formal Attire

- Mr. Photo X Media

OCT 2025
🎮 CTRL + PLAY: LITS Esports 2025
The LITS Esports event, CTRL + PLAY, was held on October 9–10, 2025. Students competed in Mobile Legends and Valorant, showing off their skills and teamwork.
🏅 Winners:
- Champion – ₱1000
- 1st Place – ₱500
Congratulations to all participants and winners for making this event a success!

🕵️‍♂️ LITS Quest 2025
LITS Quest 2025 took place on October 1, 2025, challenging students to find hidden QR codes throughout the Engineering Building.
Participants showcased their problem-solving skills and teamwork, making it an exciting and fun scavenger hunt. Congratulations to the top teams!

🌻 LITS General Assembly 2025
The LITS General Assembly was successfully held on September 16, 2025, featuring fun games, interactive sessions, and prizes for all attendees.
Thank you to everyone who joined and made the event lively and memorable!
11
SEP 2025
🤖✨ AI After Dark: Ethical Tech Frontiers
AI After Dark was held on September 11, 2025, where Sir Jherod Valdoz discussed the ethical side of Artificial Intelligence.
Students explored topics on data ethics, responsible AI, and future tech innovations. The seminar inspired participants to think critically about technology in society.

SEP 2025
🔥 IgnITe Your Tech Journey
IgnITe Your Tech Journey took place on September 9, 2025, featuring inspiring talks from Allyza Denisse Bispo and Kurt Carvey Cadenas.
Students gained insights into navigating their paths in the IT world, making it a motivating and informative session.
